About Me

I was born in Argentina, grew up in New York City, and attended school in the Bronx. With this diverse background, I've developed a deep appreciation for Argentine cuisine and often find myself craving our traditional dishes. When I'm not reminiscing about food, I'm diving into video games, getting lost in movies, or scouting diverse restaurants. Traveling is another passion—having explored 15 cities across 10 states, I'm always on the lookout for new adventures.

Throughout my journey, I've taken on some cool projects. I whipped up a simple Python code to check out blood pressure readings, making it a handy tool for folks to gauge their health. On another note, I sketched out a proposal to help USPS deliver packages quicker and cheaper – I'm all about refining everyday processes. I've also dabbled in project management, working on a simulation that gave me a sneak peek into the intricacies of that world. And lastly, on the fun-er side of projects, I planned out an entire dream vacation in a case study, weighing the pros of travel agencies against the adventure of going solo.

Currently, I'm deeply involved in a financial company that's bustling with 10,000 employees. Leading a stellar team of 25, I make sure everything entering our building finds its rightful place. While my role isn't super tech-centric, it's gratifying in its unique way. Organizing, delivering, and ensuring everyone's needs are met is my daily mantra. I've tried diving into the financial depths of our company, and trust me, it's a whole universe in itself!

To round things off: authenticity matters to me. If you're genuine, you'll find I'm all ears and ready to help. I value respect and kindness above all. Whether it's assisting someone, immersing myself in a video game, or unearthing a must-visit restaurant, I embrace life wholeheartedly. And hey, if you ever need a tip on games, movies, or mouth-watering Argentine dishes, remember, I'm just a message away!
